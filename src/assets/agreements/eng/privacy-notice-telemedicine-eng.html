U.S.A. Medical Services Corporation Privacy Notice for holders and beneficiaries of Bupa health insurance policies who receive Teladoc’s Virtual Care services

The Teladoc Virtual Care service offered on this website is provided to individuals who are holders and/or beneficiaries of certain health insurance policies issued by insurance companies that are members of the Bupa (British United Provident Association) corporate group and/or are affiliated to it, and who also legally use the Bupa Global Latin America trade name

In order to be able to receive the above-mentioned Virtual Care service, the requester of such services must grant certain consents and/or authorizations required for the collection and the treatment of his or her personal data that are necessary to receive the offered Virtual Care services. These consents and/or authorizations are the following:

  • Explicit authorization of the requester, granted to U.S.A. Medical Services Corporation (hereinafter, "USA Med") with address at 17901 Old Cutler Road, Suite 300, Palmetto Bay, FL 33157, United States of America. USA Med is an affiliated entity of the Bupa corporate group (better known in Latin America under the trade name: Bupa Global Latin America), to collect, use and process your personal data for the following purposes and activities:

    • To request, through USA Med, that Teladoc Health International, S.A. (Teladoc) provide you with its medical advisory services, called: "Virtual Care".

    • Authorize USA Med to share the requester's personal data with Teladoc, so that the requested receives Virtual Care services. Teladoc is domiciled in Vía Augusta, 252-260, 0817 Barcelona, Spain. Therefore, the requester of the Virtual Care services, authorizes USA Med to make international transfers of its personal data as necessary for USA Med to share the requester's data with Teladoc, in order to receive Virtual Care services. The personal data elements of the requester that are authorized to be shared by USA Med with Teladoc, are the following:

      • Name
      • Health insurance policy number
      • Member number
      • Date of birth
      • Country of location
      • Language
      • Telephone number, where Teladoc can contact the requester, regarding the Virtual Care service
      • E-mail address where Teladoc can contact the requester regarding the Virtual Care service
      • The date requested (when applicable) for a Virtual Care appointment

      The personal data listed above may be considered health data by the legislation of protection of personal data at some jurisdictions.

    • Authorize USA Med to receive the requester's personal data that Teladoc shares with USA Med, data that will be directly and specifically related to the result of the analysis of the requester's responses to Teladoc's customer satisfaction surveys with respect to the Virtual Care service received. USA Med is explicitly authorized by the requester to process such personal data for the following specific purposes:

      • For the purpose of analysis and changes or improvements to the service program to which the insurance policy under which the requester is subject to.
      • For internal reporting purposes of USA Med and/or of entities that are members and/or affiliates of the Bupa corporate group.

      Teladoc is responsible for the personal data database(s) of its Virtual Care service.

      Teladoc is not a member-of nor it is an affiliate-to the Bupa corporate group.

      After submitting/sending your application to receive Virtual Care services, these will always be provided directly by Teladoc to the requester, without any participation in the rendering of such services form USA Med nor from any entity that is a member-of and/or an affiliate-to the Bupa corporate group

      Please note that in general, and with the exception noted above (in number 5), the personal data you share with Teladoc in the course of the services you receive from Teladoc will not be shared with USA Med or (if applicable) your employer. USA Med will never review any personal data held by Teladoc without first obtaining explicit and sufficient consent from the data subject or his or her legal representative.

  • Explicit authorization from the requester given to Teladoc to

    • Use the requester's personal data that is detailed above as it is received from USA Med, so that Teladoc can provide the requester with its Virtual Care services.
    • Share with USA Med, personal data of the requester that will be directly and specifically referred to the result of the requester's answers to Teladoc's customer satisfaction surveys, with respect to the Virtual Care service received.
